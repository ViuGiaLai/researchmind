v0.6 Features

Built for researchers who value privacy & performance

Everything you need to manage, read, and synthesize research — all on your machine.

Import & Index

PDF, DOCX, EPUB, BibTeX, RIS, Zotero. OCR for scanned PDFs, bge-m3 embedding + ChromaDB.

Hybrid Search

BM25 (SQLite FTS5) + vector semantic + cross-encoder rerank. Saved search, filter by collection/tag/year.

RAG Chat

Streaming, multi-paper, Evidence-only mode, citation panel, DOI verification, trust panel.

Review & Screening

Insights, screening board, 7-section review builder, comparison matrix, export DOCX/Markdown.

Evidence Matrix

Extract claims by criterion, sourced quotes, visual comparison table across papers.

Graph & Brain

Paper relationship graph and personal brain — reading suggestions, tags, statistics over time.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?

Yes. ResearchMind is local-first. Your PDFs, notes, and indexes stay on your machine. Cloud AI is only used when you explicitly enable it and send a query.

Can I use ResearchMind offline?

Yes. Use local AI models (llama-server, GGUF) for fully offline operation. Library management and search work without any internet connection.

What happens to my data if I uninstall?

Local data persists in the application data directory. You can delete it from Settings or manually remove the ResearchMind folder.
