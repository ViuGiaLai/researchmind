FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I use ResearchMind for free forever?

Yes. The Free tier includes library management, import, and hybrid search indefinitely. The 14-day Pro trial gives you full access to evaluate all features.

Do I need to pay for cloud AI usage?

Pro includes cloud AI free tier usage. If you exceed the fair-use limit or bring your own API key, you pay the provider directly. ResearchMind never adds a markup on API costs.

What happens when my Pro trial ends?

Your library and data remain intact. You'll revert to Free tier features. No data is lost or locked. Upgrade anytime to regain full access.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Cancel anytime from Settings. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period. No long-term contracts.

Is there a student discount?

Yes. Students with a valid .edu email get 50% off Pro. Contact us with your student ID to activate the discount.
